About the Artist

Adriana Popovic is a sculptor whose practice explores form, balance, and the dialogue between material and space. Her work ranges from small-scale bronze pieces to large public installations. Born in [City/Country], Adriana studied at [School/Program] and has exhibited in [Cities/Regions].

Through a process-led approach, she embraces the tactile and the structural, inviting viewers to move around the work and encounter shifting relationships of light and shadow.
